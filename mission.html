Our Mission


Sky Asylum exists to shine the light of the truth of creation and our Creator through the medium of film.

It exists to hold a mirror up to the beauty, joy and pain of the everyday; reflecting back the black, white and full color spectrum of life.

Made of a team of poets, dreamers, pirates and adventurers, Sky Asylum will continue to make quality films as long as those movies are relevant and armed with purpose.

Sky Asylum is defined by no one person, or one place, but rather by the King and His Kingdom.
